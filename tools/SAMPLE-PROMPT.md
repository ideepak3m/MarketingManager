# Copy-Paste AI Prompt (Customize for Your Campaign)

---

Generate realistic social media comments and replies for a marketing campaign demo. The comments should reflect natural customer journey progression through each phase.

**Campaign Phases:**

**Phase 1: Awareness**
- Duration: November 11-17, 2024
- Goal: Introduce the product and build initial curiosity
- Customer Mindset: Just discovered the product, wants to understand what it is
- Typical Questions: "What is this?", "Who is this for?", "First time seeing this"

**Phase 2: Consideration**
- Duration: November 18-24, 2024
- Goal: Showcase features, answer objections, build trust
- Customer Mindset: Interested but evaluating options, comparing with competitors
- Typical Questions: "How does this work?", "What's the pricing?", "vs. competitors?"

**Phase 3: Conversion**
- Duration: November 25-December 1, 2024
- Goal: Drive signups, support new users, celebrate conversions
- Customer Mindset: Ready to try/buy, needs support getting started
- Typical Questions: "Just signed up!", "How do I...", "Already seeing results"

**Generate 8-10 comment/reply pairs for EACH phase.**

**Requirements:**

1. **Format as JSON object with phase names as keys:**
```json
{
  "Awareness": [
    {
      "comment": "The actual comment text - natural and conversational",
      "reply": "Brand's helpful reply - professional but friendly",
      "sentiment": "positive"
    }
  ],
  "Consideration": [...],
  "Conversion": [...]
}
```

2. **Sentiment Distribution:**
   - 40% positive (excited, appreciative, enthusiastic)
   - 40% question (curious, seeking clarification, comparing)
   - 15% neutral (observational, informational)
   - 5% negative (concerns, but constructive - keeps it realistic)

3. **Comment Characteristics:**
   - Natural social media language (casual but not unprofessional)
   - Vary length (some short reactions, some detailed questions)
   - Include emojis occasionally but don't overdo it
   - Mix of individuals and businesses commenting
   - Some comments reference specific features

4. **Reply Characteristics:**
   - Directly answer the question or acknowledge the comment
   - Provide actionable next steps where appropriate
   - Include CTAs occasionally (visit site, DM us, check email, etc.)
   - Professional but warm and approachable tone
   - Sometimes include links/references (e.g., "Check our pricing page")

5. **Phase-Specific Guidance:**

   **Awareness Phase - Focus on discovery:**
   - "Never heard of this before but looks interesting"
   - "What exactly does this do?"
   - "Is this new?"
   - "This could solve [problem]"
   - Replies should educate and welcome

   **Consideration Phase - Focus on evaluation:**
   - "How does this compare to [competitor]?"
   - "What's the pricing like?"
   - "Do you integrate with [tool]?"
   - "Is there a free trial?"
   - "Looking for [specific feature]"
   - Replies should inform and persuade

   **Conversion Phase - Focus on action:**
   - "Just signed up!"
   - "Already seeing results!"
   - "Quick question about [feature]"
   - "The onboarding was smooth"
   - "Upgraded to paid plan"
   - Replies should support and celebrate
